Full Body Workout Tips:

  • Start with a 5–10 minute dynamic warm-up to prevent injuries.
  • Use proper form over heavier weights for effective gains.
  • Balance strength training with cardio for fat loss.
  • Stay consistent – track reps and rest to progress weekly.
  • Fuel your body with protein-rich meals post workout.
