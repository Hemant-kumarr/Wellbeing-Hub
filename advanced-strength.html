Advanced Yoga - Strength

Challenge your strength, balance, and control with these advanced poses.

00:00

Firefly Pose (Tittibhasana)

Improves arm strength, core engagement, and inner thigh flexibility.

Side Crow (Parsva Bakasana)

Builds strong obliques, balance, and upper-body control.

Handstand (Adho Mukha Vrksasana)

Ultimate strength pose, enhances shoulder stability and core power.

Eight-Angle Pose (Astavakrasana)

Demands total-body strength and flexibility, especially core and arms.

Forearm Stand (Pincha Mayurasana)

Strengthens shoulders, arms, and improves body alignment awareness.

Strength Practice Tips:

  • Warm up your wrists, shoulders, and core before advanced postures.
  • Use a wall or props if needed for support during balance poses.
  • Focus on your breath to stay stable and calm.
  • Train consistently and give your body recovery time.
